    Riffle reads the Bow at Calgary gauge (Water Survey of Canada station 05BH004) for live flow and level, and Open-Meteo for weather. Trout-timing here is a heuristic — dawn/dusk, cloud cover, flow, pressure, water temp, and hatch likelihood — not a guarantee. Always check current conditions and your own safety before wading. When water is warm (above ~19–20 °C), consider not fishing: trout are badly stressed and often die after release in warm water.
