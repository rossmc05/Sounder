    Solunar theory is a century-old fishing heuristic, not a scientific guarantee — treat it as one more clue, alongside water temp, structure, and what's actually working that day. Times are calculated from the coordinates above using your device's clock, and rise/set times are accurate to within a few minutes. Live pressure, cloud, and rain conditions are looked up automatically for the selected date when possible; for dates too far out to forecast, switch any of the Conditions controls to a manual setting.
